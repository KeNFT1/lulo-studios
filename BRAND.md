**Hero Headlines (h1)**
- Size: 4rem (64px) desktop, 2.5rem (40px) mobile

- Size: 2.5rem (40px)
- Weight: Bold (700)

**Subsections (h3)**
- Size: 1.5rem (24px)
- Weight: Bold (700)
- Color: Lulo Purple

**Body Text**
- Size: 1.1rem (18px)
- Weight: Normal (400)
- Color: Light Gray (#E5E5E5)

**Secondary Text**
- Size: 1rem (16px)
- Weight: Normal (400)
- Color: Muted Gray (#A1A1AA)

## Voice & Tone

### Personality
- **Builder-focused:** We ship things that work
- **Slightly irreverent:** Not corporate, not afraid to be direct
- **Confident:** We know what we're building and why
- **Technical but accessible:** Smart without being pretentious

### Voice Principles

**✅ Do:**
- Be direct and honest
- Use technical terms when appropriate
- Show personality and humor
- Focus on utility and results
- Reference the ape connection naturally

**❌ Don't:**
- Use corporate buzzwords
- Over-explain or be condescending  
- Pretend to be something we're not
- Hide behind jargon
- Ignore the BAYC heritage

### Sample Phrases

**Taglines:**
- "Building at the intersection of AI, healthcare, and web3"
- "Useful things. Ape IP. No bullshit."
- "Built by an ape who codes"

**CTAs:**
- "See What We Build"
- "Ship with Lulo"
- "Build Something Useful"

**Descriptions:**
- "No hype. No empty promises. Just real tools that solve real problems."
- "Lulo doesn't just hold the IP — Lulo uses it."

## Usage Guidelines

### Logo Usage
- Always use the full ape image when possible
- When space is limited, use "L" monogram in Lulo Purple
- Never distort, stretch, or modify the original ape image
- Maintain clear space equal to 1/2 the logo width on all sides

### Color Usage
- Dark theme is primary - always start here
- Use Lulo Purple sparingly for maximum impact
- Maintain high contrast for accessibility
- Test all color combinations for readability

### Typography Usage
- Stick to the defined hierarchy
- Use purple for headings and accents only
- Ensure body text has sufficient contrast
- Keep line lengths readable (45-75 characters)

## File Naming

**Consistent naming for brand assets:**
- Logo: `lulo-logo.png`, `lulo-logo.svg`
- Icons: `lulo-icon-16.png`, `lulo-icon-32.png`, etc.
- Badges: `lulo-badge.svg`, `lulo-badge.png`

## Examples

### Good Usage ✅
- Clean layouts with plenty of whitespace
- Purple used for accents and highlights
- Technical content explained clearly
- Personality shows through copy
- Ape imagery used respectfully

### Bad Usage ❌
- Cluttered layouts with poor hierarchy
- Overuse of purple causing visual noise
- Corporate-speak or buzzword-heavy copy
- Hiding or minimizing the ape connection
- Poor contrast or readability
